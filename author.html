The Artist

Hello I'm James Lewis, a multidisciplinary British artist. At the age of 16 I decided to improve my handwriting by taking up calligraphy as a hobby. The hobby quickly grew into a passion for the creative process, and I was hooked.

Five years later, with a degree in graphic design, I set out to pursue my artistic practise full-time. Beginning my career as a commercial lettering artist, I slowly developed my creative style as I learned how to best produce entertaining, artistic video content. This enthusiasm for video content, social media understanding, and traditional artistic approach, have since formed the basis of my creative style.

I now share my artistic process with a global audience of over 5 million people, across Instagram and TikTok, with my videos collectively accumulating over 1 billion views. I exhibit my art, and teach workshops all over the world. Whilst working with some of the most well known brands on a range of creative projects, from video content creation and public speaking, to live mural painting, and product development.
